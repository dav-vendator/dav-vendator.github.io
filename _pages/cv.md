## Research Summary

<div class="cv-card">
I work at the intersection of <strong>Quantum Information Theory</strong>, <strong>Quantum Machine Learning</strong>, and <strong>sequential learning systems</strong>.

My research explores how quantum systems can represent memory, temporal correlations, and evolving structure through coherent dynamics. Coming from a computer science and machine learning background, I am particularly interested in bridging ideas from recurrent neural networks, temporal representation learning, and information theory with quantum systems and quantum computation.
</div>
